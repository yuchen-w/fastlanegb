About FastlaneGB

Established in 1996, Our company headquarter in China Nantong Dayu fitness co.Ltd. A professional manufacturer of all kinds of sporting goods and fitness equipment. We are famous for our good credit and quality in China. We have big markets not only in China but also in most countries all over the word. Our products include: dumbbells, chrome barbells, cast iron plates, chrome plates, electronic dartboards, fitness equipment, training sets, horse shoe sets, gym balls, jump ropes, hand grips, Yoga mats, exercise mats, wrist/angle weights, casual chairs, sleeping bags, home gyms, trampolines, mini steppers, AB sliders, benches, fitness bikes, treadmills, and messages. Most of our products have been certified by CE, RoHS, GS, TUV, SASO, UL, and PSE standards and are exported to Europe, the United States, the Middle East, and Japan. Our products are warmly welcomed by overseas consumers. We adhere to the business principle of "Best Quality and Supreme Reputations".
